DESCRIPTION

A half day symposium to explore the use of colour to express emotions and sensations. This symposium will present the outcome of a UCL Grand Challenges: Human Wellbeing award exploring how colour can express feelings and emotions, towards developing a visual language for wellbeing. The project, 'Measuring Wellbeing Creatively' has worked with a group of stroke survivors to develop a 'toolkit' for measuring wellbeing using colours. Join us to find out more about the project and to test out the tooklit we have produced. This event is open to all but may be of most interest to academics, speech therapists, students healthcare professionals, artists, stroke survivors and their families. Lunch and refreshments will be provided.
